Team

We define ourselves as a modern and multi-disciplinary team. We come from very diverse fields, from Geography to Translation, passing through Design. Nevertheless, we do have a common object: upgrade ourselves professionally to become programmers, taking the lead towards digital diversity.

Our experiences and our passion for technology has pooled us together in this project and that’s how our first collaborations have taken place. Be aware! There are still many to come!

Strengths

We are innovative, curious and prone to change. We have faith in ourselves, our work and our environment and we act accordingly. Thus, if you are looking for restless, proactive and entrepreneurial people, you have found them!

Weaknesses

Nobody is perfect… and neither are we! We have a long way to go ahead of us regarding technical knowledge. We need training, help and a little patience but, trust in us! If you can offer us that, we won’t let you down!
